What is the most effective way to sell something?

A referral from a friend.

But how do you scale that?

You can't possibly ask everybody for a referral.

Enter: Influencers.

The struggle that every brand has is getting the trust of their demographic. This is why referrals work. It bypasses the trust layer in the customer journey.

Influencer marketing creates referrals from friends at scale.

Influencers ≠ Celebrities. Celebrities are celebrated. Influencers are influential. True influencers create a connection with each and every follower. You may not recognize them at a coffee shop, but they influence purchase behavior more than any comparable form of marketing.
